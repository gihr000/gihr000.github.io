Email Protector

Cyber security should not be compromised on. But what do we actually know about cyber security?
Are we safe?

Hackers nowadays proved to be extremely creative and their favourite tool is social engineering. There are different types of attacks that could be executed, but the one most commonly known among cyber security professionals and most commonly used by cyber criminals is called phishing. Criminals infiltrate your private life, email and chat correspondence and thus, one way or another, gain access to everything that you try so hard to keep away from them - your money.

This is where our knowledge can help you. By installing Email Protector, you can be sure that the email correspondence you're leading is with the people you've already trusted.

Our Chrome extension provides new and revolutionary means to keep cyber criminals out of your correspondence. No more phishing or man in the email attacks. This extension provides you with the virtual security that you need. Click here to read more about Email Protector!
